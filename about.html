About Benayas Leulseged Software Community

Founded in 2026, BLSC is a software developer company in Ethiopia, dedicated to helping individuals and organizations build their digital presence and solve technology challenges.

We provide quality technology services including web development, software solutions, app development, and tech support. Our goal is to deliver excellent work at affordable prices while teaching and helping others learn.

Our Mission
